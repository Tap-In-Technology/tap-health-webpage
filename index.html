Why Now?

Tap is now the default gesture: contactless payments have gone mainstream, NFC is second nature, and a new generation expects to tap for everything.

Card Transactions

~70%

Contactless already drives ~70% of all US face-to-face card transactions, up from just 3% in 2017, so "tap" is now the default consumer gesture.

Consumer Adoption

80%

80% of consumers have used a phone or watch to pay by tap, signaling a broad comfort with NFC beyond payments.

Teen iPhone Users

87%

Among US teens, 87% carry an iPhone and 42% used Apple Pay last month, making "tap to transact" a banked-in habit.
