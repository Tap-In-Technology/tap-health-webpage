Our MVP

Tap Health’s early-stage system is designed to test and refine our backend before full public launch.

When you fill out our internal ‘Create a Demo’ form, it generates a unique QR code (visible only to our team) which directs you to create your own custom form.

This QR-based system is a temporary tool for internal demos and backend validation.

To fully experience the process, a Tap Health team member will guide you through it during a private session.

In production, Tap Health will enable true one-tap NFC check-ins—no scanning required.
